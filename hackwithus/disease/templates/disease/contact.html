About us

The Mission

To provide a simple and easily usable application to the society with which individuals can check their Eye health and Skin health at Zero cost.

How this is built

This application uses Image processing, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence to process the input image, calssify it and predict the health condition

Challenges faced

There are no predefined image datasets readily availible. we had to scrape the web for images, carefully examine every single image and label it.

Future plans

  • Creating a mobile application to increase the reach of application.
  • Collect more data to tune the performance of Machine learning model.
  • Collaborating with hospitals and connecting users to respective hospitals based on user health condition >
